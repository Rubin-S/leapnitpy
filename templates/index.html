What is LEAP?

NIT Puducherry's NSS unit conducts LEAP, offering free IIT-JEE and NEET training to school students with NIT tutors in Karaikal District

Why LEAP?

LEAP aims to enhance students' skills for IIT-JEE and NEET, providing free training and support to underprivileged learners.

Who LEAP?

LEAP is conducted by the NSS unit of NIT Puducherry, with NIT students serving as tutors and moderators.

Who all in the Team?

The LEAP team consists of NIT Puducherry students from the NSS unit, serving as tutors, moderators, and coordinators.

Is it Free?

Yes, the LEAP program is free for IIT-JEE and NEET aspirants from government, government-aided, and private schools in Karaikal.
